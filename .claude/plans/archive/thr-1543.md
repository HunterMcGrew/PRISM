## Decisions

- Justification Review is a distinct section in the skill file, not buried in the bullet list — it's a different review gear (necessity vs correctness) and deserves visual separation.
- The scope guard ("does not apply to the existence of new files") targets structural decisions *within* code, not whether ticket-driven files should exist. New code internals are fair game.
- Both skills get identical Justification Review content — consistency matters since they review the same codebase from different angles.
